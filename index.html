What is OCKB?

The Open Class Knowledge Base (OCKB) is an application designed to pool the knowledge for a specific class into one accessible place.

Key Features of OCKB

Users who have signed in are able to:
  • Post questions in any topic.
  • Reply to questions.
  • Request a new topic.
Users who have signed in and are also teachers or TAs can:
  • Sticky important posts.
  • Unsticky posts that are no longer relevent.
  • Delete posts.
  • Approve a new topic.
Users who want to use the site but have not logged in can explore the site, however:
  • Authorship of posts will be unknown to them
  • They cannot create posts or replies
